## Overview
This document defines the resource roles , their characterstics , typical allocation patterns and rates. 

## Resource SKUs

| SKU | Role | Responsibility |
|-----|------|----------------|
| SDM | Service Delivery Manager | Project management, coordination, status reporting |
| SA/Sr SA | Solution Architect | Advisor, trusted expert in Snowflake implementation. Design, code review, strategy. Hands-on if needed |
| SC | Solution Consultant | Hands-on development or co-development |
| MSA | Migration Solution Architect | SA with migration focus and experience |
| MSC | Migration Solution Consultant | SC with migration focus and experience |

### Senior Solution Architect 
**Seniority** : 15+ years of experiance , 5+ years with Snowflake 

**Key responsibilities** :
- Solution Architecure Design 
    - Data Architecure design 
    - Data Model Design 
- Technical Leadership
- Architecure review
- Performance optimization stratgey 
- High level design decisions 
- Role bases access control design 
- Tagging Framework
- Design security policies implementation 


**Typical Activities** :
- Architecure Design sessions 
- Technical Assessments 
- Complex Troubleshooting
- Customer presentations 
- Design review 
- Peformance analysis & generate recommendations 
- Governance Framework implementation 

**Skills** :
- Deep Snowflake expertise including advanced features 
- Multi cloud architecure 
- Data model mastery 
- Performance tuning expert
- Good understanding of Security architecure 
- Understand Enterprise patterns 
- Communication with C-level 


**Allocation Patterns** :
- Advisory : 60-80% of engagement 
- Build : 15-30% of engagement 
- Co Build : 25-40% of engagement

### Senior Delivery Manager 
**Seniority** : 10+ years of project management experiance Snowflake 

**Key responsibilities** :
- Project Planning and tracking
- Risk managenment 
- Stakeholder communication
- Resource coordination
- Budget manangment 
- Status Reporting 
- Issue esaclation and mitigation
- Project Kick-off and closure  


**Typical Activities** :
- Project planning
- Weekly status meeting  
- Change management 
- Customer relationship management  
- Team coordination
- Risk managenment  


**Skills** :
- Project Management 
- Stakeholder Management
- Risk managenment 
- Budget manangment 
- Communication with C-level 
- Snowflake feature awareness


**Allocation Patterns** :
- Small Projects (<3 months, Simple , 1-2 team members) : 15-20% of engagement 
- Medium Projects (2-6 months or Complex and 3-4 team members) : 20-25% of engagement 
- Large Projects (>6 months or  Complex  or mutiple team to manage) : 25-30% of engagement
